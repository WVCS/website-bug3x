Astrox Series

Astrox 99


Speed: 6/10

Durability: 10/10

Beginner friendly: Yes

Control: 7/10



Pros:

The most head heavy racket used by both Lee Chong Wei and Kento Momota, results in a very heavy and powerful attack

Cons:

Weight of the racket is not balanced, some players may have a hard time using and controlling the racket

Astrox 100


Speed: 9/10

Durability: 9/10

Beginner friendly: No

Control: 9/10



Pros:

This racket has the new technology of the extra slim shaft and the sleek frame, allowing for more control and a faster impact. Can be used in both singles and doubles, has excellent drive power and produces very clean, crisp shots. Head heavy racket but is very light to swing, producing quick and explosive shots. Currently used by Viktor Axelsen and Akane Yamaguchi

Cons:

The racket may be too stiff for some players, and may lead to excess fatigue in the arm. Control may be too much, which leads to some players not being able to exhibit their full power with the racket, as it requires consistent technique

Astrox 88D


Speed: 8/10

Durability: 9/10

Beginner friendly: Yes

Control: 8/10



Pros:

A very popuar racket for its all-round capabilities, very widely used by rear-court doubles players but also can be used well in singles. Has a very large sweet spot because of the isometric frame

Cons:

Not used by Lee Chong Wei
