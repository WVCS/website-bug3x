Nanoflare Series

Nanoflare 700


Speed: 9/10

Durability: 6/10

Beginner friendly: Yes

Control: 6/10



Pros:

Very beginner friendly but advanced racket, with a medium flex for more power but with all the same capabilities of a lightweight racket

Cons:

Harder to control with the lightness of the racket and speed which it can be swung

Nanoflare 1000


Speed: 9/10

Durability: 7/10

Beginner friendly: Yes

Control: 6/10



Pros:

A balanced racket, with a light frame and powerful swings. A bit harder to control, but with practice can be used as a beginner racket

Cons:

May be to hard to control for new players who have the wrong technique

Nanoflare 800 (NEW!)


Speed: 10/10

Durability: 8/10

Beginner friendly: Yes

Control: 9/10



Pros:

A very lightweight racket with all the new technology. This racket is built for fast-paced drives for doubles but can also be used very well in singles

Cons:

Harder to control with the lightness of the racket and speed which it can be swung
